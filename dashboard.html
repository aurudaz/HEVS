Limitations and oversizing risk

  • Pipe sizing is driven by the hourly peak demand (instantaneous). Short peaks inflate diameters compared to an energy‑weighted or percentile design.
  • Foisonnement is applied only once; the optimization still assumes the diversified peak occurs simultaneously on every branch.
  • Thermal losses, pump constraints, and return temperature limits are not modelled, so safety margins are absorbed by pipe sizing.
  • Resource dispatch is static (max power only) without cost or availability constraints.
